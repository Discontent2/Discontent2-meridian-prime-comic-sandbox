## Core Premise

`Worldskin` is an 8-level gamified interpretation of Project Green Glove / Worldskin Prototype logic.

Each level takes place inside a Worldskin-contained biome patch. The biomes are not untouched natural areas. They are corporate contact ecologies: selected, wrapped, stabilized, observed, and simplified into playable spaces.

Core line:

```text
The player is not crossing a world. The player is crossing the places World Works taught the world to pretend.
```

---

## Naming Structure

Each level uses two names:

```text
Player-facing biome title
World Works internal designation
```

This lets the game remain readable while preserving the corporate containment layer.

## Worldskin Visual Layer

Every level should include visible evidence of the containment membrane.

Recurring Worldskin elements:

```text
membrane seams
solar-thread mesh
pearl ceramic nodes
photosynthetic panels
rootlike sensory tendrils
signal veins
sampling blisters
translucent membrane tension
soft green glow becoming wrong
amber pressure glass
ceramic restraint ribs
field anchors
garden-lab structures that are actually clamps
```

These elements should be visible but not always explained.

## Level Notes

### 1. Null Cavern / WS-01 Subterranean Contact Patch

The first playable Worldskin patch. A cave system wrapped in an interface membrane. Wet stone, alien fungal glow, dead console green, black mineral walls, ceramic contact nodes tucked into rock.

Gamified purpose:

```text
teach movement
teach combat spacing
introduce membrane seams
establish hidden passages
establish that the cave is not fully natural
```

Boss candidate:

```text
The Blind Gate Larva
```

---

### 2. Rust Marsh / WS-02 Wetland Corrosion Patch

A Worldskin wetland sample where industrial material and swamp ecology have become inseparable. Metal reeds, black water, sinking machinery, floating membrane pylons, and dead monitoring pods half-submerged in rot.

Gamified purpose:

```text
slow traversal
poison pools
sinking platforms
half-failed containment anchors
lurking enemies without creature-call BGM
```

Boss candidate:

```text
Siltjaw Clampbeast
```

---

### 3. Relay Spires / WS-03 Vertical Signal Ecology

A vertical Worldskin patch built around signal-bearing structures. Antenna trunks, storm towers, service platforms, magnetic lifts, and signal veins crawling over exposed height.

Gamified purpose:

```text
vertical climb
wind/current hazards
moving lifts
membrane exposed to storm pressure
signal distortion without UI-like audio cues
```

Boss candidate:

```text
The Weather Antenna
```

---

### 4. Furnace District / WS-04 Thermal Industrial Envelope

A contact patch where heat, industry, and containment logic fuse into a playable foundry. Molten channels, conveyor ruins, smoke stacks, old worker tunnels, thermal ribs, and overheating garden-lab clamps.

Gamified purpose:

```text
moving belts
heat gates
crushers
thermal timing puzzles
containment systems failing under pressure
```

Boss candidate:

```text
Kilnback Executor
```

---

### 5. Blueglass Reef / WS-05 Flooded Crystal Sample

A flooded Worldskin sample with alien aquatic ruins, crystal coral, pressure doors, floating debris, soft bioluminescence, and pearl sensor nodes embedded in reef growth.

Gamified purpose:

```text
swimming / low gravity movement
pressure gates
slow enemy drift
beautiful containment horror
controlled observation disguised as wonder
```

Boss candidate:

```text
Pearl Circuit Leviathan
```

---

### 6. Graveyard Arcade / WS-06 Civic Debris Interface Zone

A dead civic/entertainment environment wrapped inside Worldskin logic. Broken cabinets, signboards, prize machines, shuttered malls, dead escalators, rain on blacktop, and membrane panels pretending to be old storefront glass.

Gamified purpose:

```text
maze-like city stage
fake exits
corrupted signage
interface residue
avoid coin, menu, and power-up audio in BGM
```

Boss candidate:

```text
Attract Mode Wraith
```

---

### 7. Orbital Rootworks / WS-07 Elevated Root-Machine Lattice

A late-game Worldskin patch in an elevated or orbital structure. Roots through metal, organic cables, gravity tricks, solar ribs, sterile pad pressure, half garden and half server rack.

Gamified purpose:

```text
gravity shifts
branching paths
biotech-machine enemies
late-game movement mastery
containment membrane visibly straining
```

Boss candidate:

```text
The Photosynthetic Lock
```

---

### 8. The Enum Core / WS-08 Core Membrane Nexus

The final game-realm expression of the Worldskin system. A code-cathedral machine-organism wrapped in black glass, blue light, red status glyphs, pulsing organic circuits, and a darker reprise of Null Cavern's membrane language.

Gamified purpose:

```text
final gauntlet
last locks
boss approach
escape sequence
make the player realize Null Cavern was the first mouth of the same system
```

Boss candidate:

```text
The Root Error
```

---

## Design Rule

Every level should feel like:

```text
natural biome
plus corporate containment
plus readable 16-bit gameplay
plus something underneath refusing to be modeled
```
