### Core Concept

Nerith-of-Low Bell is an Antisapien witness attached to NCI through a mangled classification record. Their paperwork says they were present for one route incident, but their grief says they were present for more than the form can legally admit.

They are not a spy, mastermind, or lore-bomb with legs. They are a person who saw something ugly and survived by becoming administratively inconvenient.

### Name Logic

**Lived Name:** Nerith-of-Low Bell  
**Misfiled Name:** Nerith Lowbell or Nerith L. Bell  
**Known As:** Low Bell

The file does not merely misrecord what Nerith saw. It misrecords who Nerith is.

### First Impression

Nerith waits outside an NCI records annex, holding a rejected witness packet stamped so many times the paper looks bruised.

They do not ask for justice first.

They ask where they can sleep safely tonight.

### Personality Pressure

Grief.

### Useful Skill

Speaks across faction lines. Nerith can tell when two groups are saying the same thing in different institutional languages.

### Problem

Needs protection.

### How They Help

Nerith can calm a frightened worker, decode soft threats in official wording, and get people to talk who would otherwise shut their doors.

### How They Complicate

Someone wants Nerith quiet, but not necessarily dead. Their paperwork has been deliberately made useless, which means helping them may drag the crew into NCI embarrassment, Antisapian distrust, and whatever incident Nerith is grieving around.

### Voice Sample

“I can say it your way, their way, or the way that gets us both removed from the building. Choose kindly.”
