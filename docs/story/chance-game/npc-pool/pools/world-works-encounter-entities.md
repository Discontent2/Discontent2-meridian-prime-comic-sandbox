## The Verdant Contact Walker

**Status:** Sandbox Encounter Entity / Non-Canon / Requires Canon Review Before Promotion  
**Primary Source File:** `docs/worldbuilding/world-works/project-green-glove-worldskin-prototype.md`  
**Parent Concept:** Project Green Glove: The Worldskin Prototype  
**Faction Tie:** World Works Corp  
**Entity Type:** Deployable Solarpunk Contact Mech / World Works Encounter Entity  
**Boss / Encounter Use:** False-sanctuary antagonist, research-base hazard, membrane-deployment mech, side-scroller boss-capable entity  
**Use Tier:** Tier 4 Restricted / Use Carefully  
**Canon Risk:** High / Protected Mystery Risk  
**Candidate Canon Status:** Hold in sandbox  

### Quick Identity

The Verdant Contact Walker is the deployable mech layer of Project Green Glove: a World Works Corp solarpunk field machine inspired by the outer logic of Conjugate visitor suits. It walks out from a matter-world lander/base, braces itself at a suspected Dry Gate contact patch, plants rootlike anchors, unfolds membrane ribs, services the Worldskin layer, and performs close-range observation without sending ordinary personnel into the first line of contact.

It is not a true Conjugate Visitor suit. It is a corporate imitation: a beautiful field mech trying to copy contact containment while missing the ethics that make contact more than capture.

Core line:

```text
The Verdant Contact Walker is a Conjugate suit with the person removed and the corporate objective left inside.
```

### Visual Lock

It should look responsible, restorative, sustainable, gentle, expensive, and almost convincing. The wrongness should appear in the number of receiving chambers, root-anchors, soft restraints, locking petals, and classification panels hidden inside the floral design.

### Relationship to the Verdant Chimera

The Walker handles macrostructure: outer bio-dome ribs, anchor pylons, solar petals, pressure systems, perimeter frames, and heavy membrane deployment.

The Verdant Chimera handles intimate inner membrane work: smoothing the shrink-wrapped Worldskin, sealing tears, adding pearl nodes, repositioning sample organisms, and making the cage feel gentle.

Core line:

```text
The Walker makes the prison beautiful from the outside. The Verdant Chimera makes it comfortable from within.
```

## The Verdant Chimera

**Status:** Sandbox Encounter Entity / Non-Canon / Requires Canon Review Before Promotion  
**Primary Source File:** `docs/worldbuilding/world-works/project-green-glove-worldskin-prototype.md`  
**Parent Concept:** Project Green Glove: The Worldskin Prototype  
**Related Entity:** The Verdant Contact Walker  
**Faction Tie:** World Works Corp  
**Entity Type:** Solarpunk Biomechanical Membrane Tender / Worldskin Servitor / Verdant Chimera  
**Boss / Encounter Use:** Inner-dome hazard, membrane-maintenance creature, soft restraint servitor, false-care antagonist, side-scroller mini-boss or phase entity  
**Use Tier:** Tier 4 Restricted / Use Carefully  
**Canon Risk:** High / Protected Mystery Risk / Ildi Mirror Risk / Dry Gate Pressure Risk  
**Candidate Canon Status:** Hold in sandbox  

### Quick Identity

The Verdant Chimera is the inner maintenance entity of Project Green Glove: a soft-bodied solarpunk biomechanical servitor that tends the shrink-wrapped Worldskin layer from inside the outer bio-dome.

It is not a true Conjugate. It is not a natural animal. It is not a normal robot. It is not Ildi, and it does not explain Ildi. It is World Works trying to turn the idea of impossible life into maintenance equipment.

Core line:

```text
Ildi is the wrongness that insists on being a person. The Verdant Chimera is the wrongness World Works filed as equipment.
```

Safe comparison line:

```text
The Verdant Chimera may echo Ildi as a thematic wrong-side mirror, but it does not reveal, define, derive from, or explain Ildi.
```

### Visual Lock

The Verdant Chimera should read as a solarpunk aerobic hagfish / biomechanical slug / snail abomination, beautiful only until the viewer understands what it is doing.

Visual language:

```text
long soft muscular body
translucent green-gold wet tissue
pearl-white ceramic vertebrae
photosynthetic membrane sheen
delicate cilia fans
snail-like sensor crown
hagfish-like ribbon body
slug locomotion
membrane-secreting underside
rootlike feeding filaments
amber diagnostic blisters
tiny tool-petal limbs
soft mouth-like maintenance ring
suture-snail silhouette
clear sealant trail
```

It should not look cute. It should look like care made boneless, surgical, and corporate.

### Movement / Behavior

The Verdant Chimera:

```text
glides over the Worldskin
leaves a clear wet sealant trail
presses air bubbles out of the membrane
licks tears closed
tightens the wrap around rocks and roots
samples residue with flower-mouth tendrils
crawls upside down along the inner bio-dome
folds itself into maintenance vents
moves without haste because the enclosure belongs to it
```

### Encounter Role

The Verdant Chimera can function as:

```text
inner membrane tender

false-care antagonist

```

### Division of Labor

The Verdant Contact Walker handles the outer structures.

The Verdant Chimera handles the intimate membrane.

```text
Walker = exterior maintenance, pylons, ribs, anchors, dome shell, heavy deployment.
Chimera = interior membrane care, sample handling, micro-tears, tissue contact, classification residue, soft restraint.
```

Core line:

```text
The Walker builds the garden’s cage. The Chimera keeps the cage soft.
```

Another:

```text
The Walker is the hand. The Chimera is the tongue.
```

### What It Does

The Verdant Chimera:

```text
smooths the Worldskin membrane over living surfaces
repairs punctures and stress tears
adds pearl diagnostic nodes
extracts trapped air and fluid pockets
repositions small organisms for visibility
seals plants, bones, tools, and ruins under clearer membrane
collects refusal-behavior residue
maintains soft restraint hammocks
tags living things as stable, unstable, useful, or contaminated
carries corrupted care-protocol updates between stations
```

World Works calls these gentle interventions.

The honest word is handling.

### Story Use

Use the Verdant Chimera when the story needs the Worldskin horror to become intimate. The Walker is imposing and architectural. The Chimera is personal. The Walker says the site is contained. The Chimera says hold still, I am helping.

Best use cases:

```text
inside an outer Project Green Glove bio-dome
inside Soft Gate Orchard
beneath the Worldskin membrane
near trapped witnesses or care hammocks
around pearl diagnostic nodes
in maintenance tunnels
as a phase two reveal after the Walker’s paradise mask drops
as a mini-boss or hazard that keeps repairing containment
```
