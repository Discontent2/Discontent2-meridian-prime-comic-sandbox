## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This document defines how intoxicants, medicines, ritual foods, and controlled substances should appear on maps and route charts.

These maps should not simply mark where substances grow.

They should show:

- verified harvest areas
- medical harvest zones
- route-prohibited substances
- toxic lookalike zones
- civic quarantine zones
- sacred guest-law foods
- contraband routes
- festival harvest corridors
- pirate vice currents
- emergency medicine caches
- false-light or hallucination hazards

---

## Core Cartographic Principle

**Candidate Canon Seed**

A substance map should answer five questions:

1. **What is harvested here?**
2. **Who is allowed to name it safe?**
3. **What happens if it is taken in the wrong season or jurisdiction?**
4. **Who smuggles, blesses, bans, or verifies it?**
5. **What route becomes more dangerous because someone used it?**

Core line:

**A cup on Meridian Prime is never only a cup. It is a route permit with a pulse.**

**Development Apocrypha / Do Not Promote Yet**

| Layer | Use |
|---|---|
| **Verified Medicinal Harvest Zone** | safe medical or restorative harvest recognized by local authority |
| **Route-Readiness Stimulant Zone** | legal or tolerated stimulant foods for workers, crews, or dock labor |

| **Civic Quarantine Zone** | Hydropolis record, signal, identity, or archive contamination risk |
| **Guest-Law Ritual Food Zone** | Craton foods and drinks tied to threshold permission |

| **Illegal Sedation / Sabotage Marker** | Bellpod Sleep, Drowncap Quiet, Tar Sleep, or similar lore markers |
| **Emergency Medicine Cache** | controlled route medicine, frost salve, mineral steep, hydration support |

## Seasonal Map Treatment

### Dry Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Dry-season maps should emphasize:

- route stimulants
- dry medicinal harvest
- convoy ration legality
- emergency mineral steeps
- highland warming teas
- illicit vision foods in desert heat
- false-distance hallucinogens
- Free Scab dry contraband markets

Map note:

**Dry season makes stimulants useful and hallucinations lethal.**

### Cataract Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Cataract-season maps should emphasize:

- descent-prohibited substances
- canyon guide ritual foods
- mistborne spore hazards
- substances that distort water sounds
- early harvest of spray foods
- route closures around impaired crews

Map note:

**Before the falls wake, every cup must answer for balance.**

### Haboon Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Haboon-season maps should emphasize:

- low-visibility intoxication risk
- false rescue lights
- spore-dust interactions
- storm smuggling routes
- emergency medicine shortages
- pirate vice currents through storm cover
- substances that distort light, sound, or distance

Map note:

**Haboon weather turns bad judgment into geography.**

### Wet Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Wet-season maps should emphasize:

- aquatic medicines
- Hydropolis verified food and drink
- wet-season fermented trade
- Free Scab pirate grog routes
- Craton wet-gate ritual drinks
- Blackstep docking restrictions
- false harbor intoxicants
- sedatives forbidden near water

Map note:

**When the roads drown, contraband learns to float.**

- Prime Ops confiscating culturally necessary stimulants
- Hydropolis rejecting cargo over origin uncertainty
- Craton refusing an improperly timed gift
- Free Scabs smuggling Drift or Black Signal
- Charterhold Reclaimers organizing over Fog Pear Cider

- false rescue lights tied to Procession Light panic
- Soft Hands distinguishing rescue lamps from Bride fields
- World Works patenting or relabeling local medicine

## Protected Mystery Guardrails

**Protected Mystery Guardrail**

Substance maps may deepen law, culture, vice, medicine, ritual, and route danger.

They must not casually reveal:

- Dry Port truth
- Core mystery
- gateway mechanics
- Thunder Egg / Stone Heart truth
- exact Craton sacred interior geography
- Rob's full death-chain implications
- Horus Skelton's full plan
- whether Archive Mold preserves literal memory

Substances may generate clues, rumors, or contradictions without becoming mystery keys.
