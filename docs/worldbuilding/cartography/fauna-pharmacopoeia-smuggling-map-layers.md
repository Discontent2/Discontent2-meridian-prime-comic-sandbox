## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This file defines how fauna-derived black market pharmacopoeia should appear on maps and route charts.

These maps should not merely show contraband routes.

They should show the moral difference between:

- shed harvest
- scavenged remains
- ethical local use
- counterfeit medicine
- poaching wounds
- sacred-body theft
- civic-origin violation
- pirate medical markets
- corporate deniable research corridors

---

## Core Cartographic Principle

**Candidate Canon Seed**

A fauna pharmacopoeia map should answer five questions:

1. **What animal was involved?**
2. **Was the material shed, scavenged, or stolen?**
3. **Who verifies the origin?**
4. **What authority is violated if the route crosses this boundary?**
5. **What damage does the trade leave behind?**

Core line:

**A contraband medicine route is also a wound map.**

---

## Map Layer Families

**Development Apocrypha / Do Not Promote Yet**

| Layer | Use |
|---|---|
| **Shed-Harvest Corridor** | legal or tolerated material gathered from naturally shed animal parts |
| **Scavenged Remains Zone** | ambiguous salvage from dead animals, wrecks, or seasonal die-off |
| **Poaching Wound Zone** | areas where animal populations, nests, or sacred corridors are harmed |
| **Sacred Body Alarm** | Craton Weight Theft or threshold-fauna contraband risk |
| **Civic-Origin Violation** | Hydropolis protected fauna / verified-origin breach |
| **Red Table Route** | Free Scab black medical market movement |
| **Counterfeit Cure Market** | zones where fake or adulterated fauna medicine circulates |
| **Route Performance Contraband** | substances used to push workers, drivers, pilots, or mechanics beyond safe limits |
| **Black Clinic Node** | illicit treatment location, not necessarily villainous |
| **Corporate Research Shadow** | World Works or affiliate deniable collection / research corridor |

## Protected Mystery Guardrails

**Protected Mystery Guardrail**

Fauna pharmacopoeia maps may deepen ecology, crime, sacred law, medicine, and route danger.

They must not casually reveal:

- Dry Port truth
- Core mystery
- gateway mechanics
- Thunder Egg / Stone Heart truth
- exact Craton sacred interior geography
- Rob's full death-chain implications
- Horus Skelton's full plan
- whether any fauna-derived substance has true signal or memory effects beyond contested claims

Do not make every contraband route secretly point to a protected mystery.
