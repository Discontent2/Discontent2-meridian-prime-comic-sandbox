## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This file defines the cartographic bias of **The Free Scabs**.

The Free Scabs do not map territory like a state.

They map opportunity, abandonment, patrol rhythm, seasonal access, hidden debt, safe betrayal, and places where official maps stop caring.

---

## Core Map Bias

**Candidate Canon Seed**

The Free Scabs map **what the world discards and what can still move**.

Where World Works maps assets, Prime Ops maps supportability, Bought Nation maps owed belonging, Hydropolis maps signal identity, and the Craton maps thresholds, the Free Scabs map:

- gaps
- patrol intervals
- abandoned camps
- unclaimed fuel
- dead channels
- weak witnesses
- safe lies
- seasonal hideouts
- salvageable wrecks
- people left behind
- roads that disappear from official concern
- routes that exist only if you are willing to be illegal

Primary question:

**What can be crossed, taken, hidden, rescued, sold, or survived after the official map gives up?**

## What Free Scab Maps Misread

**Contradictory Account**

Free Scab maps may misread:

- any law as ownership theft
- any patrol as predation
- any closed route as opportunity
- any rescue as recruitment
- any cargo as debt payment
- any clean record as cover
- any gate as insult
- any protected mystery as leverage

Their maps are brilliant for survival and terrible for trust.

## Map Type: Moving Territory

**Candidate Canon Seed**

The Free Scabs should not own fixed territory in the conventional sense.

They own moving windows:

- two nights before a patrol route refresh
- six hours before cataract surge
- the shadow of a dead relay during signal weather
- a basin track visible only after dry wind
- a smuggler road that exists until the first floodsheet
- a stolen permit valid until someone reads it carefully
- a hidden camp abandoned before the map arrives

Their territory is time plus nerve.

## Seasonal Map Logic

### Dry Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Free Scab activity increases.

Map emphasis:

- exposed scabland tracks
- mobile camps
- active Scab Cathedral windows
- convoy interception routes
- salvage fields
- hidden water caches
- dead-channel navigation lines

Status label:

**Free Scab Activity: Mobile / Active / Assume Witnessed**

### Cataract Season

**Development Apocrypha**

Free Scab routes become dangerous but valuable.

Map emphasis:

- evacuation prices
- flood-escape tracks
- cataract cutoffs
- unstable caches
- rescue or ransom opportunities
- route break ambush points

Status label:

**Free Scab Activity: Fragmented / Local Knowledge Dominant**

### Haboon Season

**Development Apocrypha**

Storms conceal movement but can trap Scab groups.

Map emphasis:

- dust-wall cover
- floodsheet hazards
- RF chaos
- storm refuges
- zero-visibility routes
- convoy distress interception

Status label:

**Free Scab Activity: Unreliable / High Risk / High Concealment**

### Wet Season

**Development Apocrypha**

Free Scabs disperse, go dormant, shift to waterborne caches, or hide in upper chambers and allied settlements.

Map emphasis:

- submerged caches
- boat routes
- dormant base states
- hidden upper refuges
- off-season contacts
- wet-season black markets

Status label:

**Free Scab Activity: Dormant / Submerged / Not Absent**

## Scab Cathedral on Maps

**Protected Mystery Guardrail / Development Apocrypha**

The Scab Cathedral should not appear as a fixed location on most maps.

Possible treatments:

- absent entirely
- shown as several false candidate sites
- marked by access windows rather than coordinates
- shown as a route convergence that moves by season
- encoded in dead-channel paths
- hidden in graffiti maps
- mislabeled as flood hazard, sinkfield, or dead camp

Free Scab maps may not say:

**Here is the Scab Cathedral.**

They may say:

**The Cathedral hears from here when the water is low.**

## Relationship to Other Map Biases

### Compared to Bought Nation Maps

Bought Nation maps show what is owed.

Free Scab maps show what can be taken because no one paid.

### Compared to Prime Ops Maps

Prime Ops maps route support.

Free Scab maps route neglect.

### Compared to World Works Maps

World Works maps assets.

Free Scab maps what leaks from the asset system.

### Compared to Hydropolis Maps

Hydropolis maps identity and signal.

Free Scab maps how to survive without being cleanly identified.

### Compared to Craton Maps

Craton maps threshold.

Free Scab maps every place a person crawls under one.
