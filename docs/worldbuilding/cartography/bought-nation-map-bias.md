## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This file defines the cartographic bias of **The Bought Nation**.

The Bought Nation's maps should differ from World Works, Prime Ops, NCI, and generic Meridian maps.

They are maps made by people trying to prove that settlement, labor, death, memory, and promise can become territorial claim.

---

## Core Map Bias

**Candidate Canon Seed**

The Bought Nation maps **owed belonging**.

Where World Works maps assets, where Prime Ops maps supportability, and where NCI maps survivable routes, Bought Nation maps show:

- promised land
- leased land
- stolen promise
- worked routes
- family districts
- charter fragments
- debt zones
- memorial roads
- fuel dependency
- contract boundaries
- places Earth forgot
- places World Works counts
- places the Bought Nation remembers itself into being

Primary question:

**What has been worked, promised, paid for, buried, and still not recognized?**

## What Bought Nation Maps Misread

**Contradictory Account**

Bought Nation maps may misread:

- corporate logistics as deliberate malice even when partly practical
- Prime Ops emergency authority as corporate control, even when life-saving
- NCI contract participation as betrayal, even when NCI families share the wound
- Hydropolis signal restrictions as class judgment
- Craton threshold critique as contempt
- ecological closures as anti-settlement policy
- survey redactions as proof of ownership theft, even when they may hide other mysteries

The maps are emotionally truthful but not neutral.

No Meridian Prime map is neutral.

## Major Map Layers

### 1. Promise Layer

**Mythic Development / Political Development**

Marks areas promised in settlement charters, migrant speeches, early compacts, or family archives.

Visual style:

- faded gold outlines
- charter-script borders
- broken promise seals
- handwritten annotations

Meaning:

**This was said to be ours.**

### 2. Lease Layer

**Corporate Distortion / Political Development**

Marks World Works lease, housing, water, fuel, and infrastructure dependencies.

Visual style:

- gray grids
- ledger boxes
- contract stamps
- recurring payment marks

Meaning:

**This is how ownership remains elsewhere.**

### 3. Labor Layer

**Development Apocrypha**

Marks where Bought Nation people built, repaired, carried, descended, shoveled, froze, bled, or died.

Visual style:

- route stitches
- wrench marks
- boot paths
- maintenance scars
- death dots
- handprint markers

Meaning:

**This place exists because our work kept it alive.**

### 4. Memory Layer

**Mythic Development**

Marks family graves, old arrival sites, abandoned camps, schoolhouses, route songs, memorial bridges, and places where names are preserved against ledger erasure.

Visual style:

- candle dots
- stitched name-lines
- mural symbols
- family color marks

Meaning:

**No ledger can erase what is named aloud.**

### 5. Dependency Chain Layer

**Political Development**

Marks fuel, water, medical, housing, communication, and evacuation chains controlled by World Works or Prime Ops.

Visual style:

- red dependency arrows
- lock marks
- ration nodes
- supply-chain links

Meaning:

**Survival is routed through someone else's hand.**

## Map Types

### Reclaimer Wall Maps

**Political Development**

Large public or hidden maps showing promised land vs leased land.

Purpose:

- organize protest
- teach history
- expose broken charters
- show dependency chains

### Route-Family Memory Maps

**Mythic Development / Development Apocrypha**

Family-kept maps showing who worked which road, who died where, which camps saved lives, and which officials lied.

Purpose:

- preserve names
- train younger route workers
- remember debt in human form

### Charter Fragment Maps

**Suppressed Archive**

Maps reconstructed from old settlement charters, land promises, erased leases, and archived World Works commitments.

Purpose:

- legal challenge
- Reclaimer legitimacy
- historical proof

### Settlement Survival Maps

**Development Apocrypha**

Practical maps used by communities to survive snowfield, dry season, and supply interruptions.

Purpose:

- water
- shelter
- fuel
- evacuation
- medical movement

These may be closer to Prime Ops maps but with more memory and distrust.

## Relationship to Other Map Biases

### Compared to World Works Maps

World Works maps ownership.

The Bought Nation maps owedness.

### Compared to Prime Ops Maps

Prime Ops maps survival logistics.

The Bought Nation maps who survival depends on and who gets credit.

### Compared to NCI Maps

NCI maps field truth and repair memory.

The Bought Nation maps political memory and settlement claim.

### Compared to Hydropolis Maps

Hydropolis maps identity, signal, and record continuity.

The Bought Nation maps identity damaged by contract and survival dependency.

### Compared to Craton Maps

Craton maps thresholds and gate-right.

The Bought Nation maps the pain of not owning its own gates.
