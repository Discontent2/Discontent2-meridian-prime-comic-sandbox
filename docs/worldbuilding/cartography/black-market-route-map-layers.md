## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This document defines how broader black market goods should appear on Meridian Prime maps and route charts.

Black market maps should not only mark smuggling routes.

They should also show:

- survival gaps
- record suppression
- sacred violations
- false safety
- illegal scanning
- forbidden evidence
- counterfeit rescue
- Hydropolis origin crimes
- Craton threshold breaches
- World Works liability shadows
- Free Scab market currents

---

## Core Cartographic Principle

**Candidate Canon Seed**

A black market route map should answer five questions:

1. **What moves here that official maps deny?**
2. **Who needs it badly enough to risk buying it?**
3. **Who is harmed if it moves?**
4. **Who is harmed if it does not move?**
5. **What official silence made the route profitable?**

Core line:

**A black market map is where the official map develops a guilty shadow.**

## Protected Mystery Guardrails

**Protected Mystery Guardrail**

Black market route maps may deepen survival, politics, record conflict, sacred law, and criminal geography.

They must not casually reveal:

- Dry Port truth
- Core mystery
- gateway mechanics
- Thunder Egg / Stone Heart truth
- exact Craton sacred interior geography
- Rob's full death-chain implications
- Horus Skelton's full plan
- exact Contact Frame / Mirror-Shell mechanics
- whether any sacred relic is authentic

Black market routes can pass near mysteries without solving them.
