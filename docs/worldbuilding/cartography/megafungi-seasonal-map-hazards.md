## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This file defines how Meridian Prime mega-fungi should appear on maps and route charts.

Mega-fungi should not be treated as decorative mushrooms.

They are:

- route-softening agents
- wreck-eaters
- corpse landmarks
- archive contaminants
- signal hazards
- sporefall weather
- false terrain
- dock rot
- hidden-water indicators
- food sovereignty resources
- pirate concealment fields
- sacred pressure ecology

---

## Core Cartographic Rule

**Candidate Canon Seed**

A Meridian Prime mega-fungi mark should answer three questions:

1. **What dead, broken, drowned, contaminated, or forgotten thing is feeding it?**
2. **How does its bloom change route safety, signal clarity, or structural reliability?**
3. **Who treats it as contamination, food, warning, witness, medicine, trap, or sacred response?**

Core line:

**Fungi mark where the map has begun to digest itself.**

## Seasonal Marking Rules

### Dry Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Dry-season maps should emphasize:

- dormant crusts
- buried mycelium
- spore dust
- false hard surfaces
- road weakening
- hidden water
- carcass-cycle blooms
- contamination of depots, vehicles, ropes, and archives

Best map note:

**Dry rot is still rot. It is only waiting for permission.**

### Cataract Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Cataract-season maps should emphasize:

- mistborne spore spread
- canyon fungal curtains
- wet brake / rope contamination
- acoustic warning distortion
- route-softening around newly active channels
- sudden bloom from rising humidity

Best map note:

**When the falls breathe, the walls bloom.**

### Haboon Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Haboon-season maps should emphasize:

- spore dust mixed with storm dust
- black slicks hidden by floodsheet water
- false beacon / visibility complications around fungal bloom fields
- contaminated shelters
- engine and filter fouling
- pirate use of fungal concealment

Best map note:

**The storm carries sand, water, and whatever learned to grow between them.**

### Wet Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Wet-season maps should emphasize:

- bloom fields
- fungal slicks
- submerged archive risk
- tower ecology
- dock rot
- hull contamination
- glowing death landmarks
- floating mats
- spore towers
- food / medicine fungal gardens

Best map note:

**When the roads drown, fungi decide what the water inherits.**

## Protected Mystery Guardrails

**Protected Mystery Guardrail**

Mega-fungi maps may deepen ecology, decay, archives, route danger, sacred geography, and piracy concealment.

They must not casually reveal:

- whether Archive Mold preserves literal memory
- exact signal relationship between fungi and Hydropolis systems
- full Craton sacred interior geography
- Thunder Egg / Stone Heart truth
- full Core mystery
- Dry Port truth
- gateway mechanics
- Rob's full death-chain implications
- Horus Skelton's full plan

Do not make every fungal network secretly intelligent.

Do not make every contamination event supernatural.
