## What the Antisapien Map Misreads

**Contradictory Account**

The Antisapien map is powerful but not omniscient.

It may misread:

- Craton sovereign silence as suspicious concealment
- human machine noise as intentional deception
- ordinary cargo grime as identity contamination
- old signals as meaningful when they may be residue
- quiet equipment as threat rather than good maintenance
- pressure-ritual restrictions as signal denial rather than sacred law

The Antisapien map hears the route.

It does not always understand why the route chooses silence.
