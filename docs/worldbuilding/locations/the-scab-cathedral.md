## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This file develops **The Scab Cathedral**, the hidden headquarters of The Free Scabs.

It should not feel like a normal fortress.

It should feel like a seasonal hiding system built inside terrain that official maps hate, misunderstand, or mark as unusable.

---

## Core Concept

**Candidate Canon Seed**

The Scab Cathedral is a hidden headquarters inside an ancient flood-scoured scabland basin formation: part canyon, part dry cave city, part abandoned wet-season ruin, part geode-collapse shelter, part stolen Traverse camp grown feral.

It only functions as headquarters during certain dry-season or low-water windows.

In wet season, it may be submerged, sealed, inaccessible, disguised by water, or transformed into a different kind of hazard.

Core line:

**The Scab Cathedral is not hidden because no one mapped it. It is hidden because every map gives up differently.**

## Why It Is Hard to Find

**Development Apocrypha**

Each major map tradition fails to find or fully hold the Scab Cathedral for different reasons.

| Map Tradition | Failure Mode |
|---|---|
| Meridian / Prime Ops | satellite and route maps classify it as dead flood terrain or unsupported scabland hazard |
| World Works | risk models mark it too unstable for permanent infrastructure, unless special black surveys exist |
| Hydropolis / Antisapien | mineral crust, stolen signal veils, and dead-channel interference scatter or misdirect readings |
| Craton / Aeonolacertian | pressure maps may mark the area as a wound to avoid rather than a site to occupy |
| Bought Nation | memory maps know rumors but not stable access windows |
| NCI / Traverse | old hands may know approaches but disagree on whether to report them |
| Free Scabs | know it by season, weather, scars, and approach ritual, not fixed coordinates |

## Seasonal States

### Dry Season

**Candidate Canon Seed**

The Scab Cathedral is most accessible during dry season.

State:

- dry approaches open
- scabland tracks usable
- hidden chambers occupied
- The Throat active
- fuel / water caches stocked
- raid planning increases
- black market thrives

Map status:

**Free Scab Access Window: Active / Unconfirmed**

### Cataract Season

**Development Apocrypha**

The Cathedral becomes dangerous and partially sealed.

State:

- dry cataract slots begin running
- lower chambers flood unpredictably
- approaches change by the day
- sound masks movement
- caches may be swept away or deliberately released

Map status:

**Access Compromised / Local Knowledge Required**

### Haboon Season

**Development Apocrypha**

The Cathedral becomes both refuge and trap.

State:

- dust walls and flood pulses hide movement
- zero visibility shields some approaches
- flash floods can cut exits
- RF chaos helps concealment but harms coordination
- leadership may use Haboon conditions for deniable movement

Map status:

**Storm Refuge / Entrapment Risk**

### Wet Season

**Development Apocrypha**

The Cathedral is mostly inaccessible or transformed.

Possible states:

- submerged
- sealed by water pressure
- reachable only by hidden boat route
- abandoned except for upper chambers
- disguised as ordinary flooded basin ruin
- used as underwater cache rather than headquarters

Map status:

**Submerged / Dormant / Do Not Confirm**
