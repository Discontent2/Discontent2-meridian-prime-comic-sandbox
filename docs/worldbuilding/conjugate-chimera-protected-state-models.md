## Purpose

Develop protected-state analogy models for Conjugate Chimeras without over-explaining them or making them common.

This file protects the canon distinction:

- Antisapiens are matter-compatible áæbsconditian phenotype people produced by Meridian Prime after first contact with Àæonos.
- True Àæonos-origin visitors require Conjugate Contact Frames / Mirror-Shells in matter environments.
- Conjugate Chimeras are rare contained bi-phase anomalies or survivors.
- Ildi Vey's Chimera status is restricted canon and should not become early exposition.

Core rule:

**A Chimera survives because the boundary is maintained, not because the contradiction is solved.**

## Core Chimera Model

A Conjugate Chimera is not a stable hybrid species.

A Chimera is a rare living boundary event.

Possible internal model:

```text
matter-compatible body / phenotype
  +
conjugate-origin phase or inheritance
  +
boundary-maintained separation
  +
biological / signal / containment adaptation
  =
rare protected-state survivor
```

The contradiction is not erased.

It is partitioned, translated, corrected, or kept apart inside one life.

## Protected Boundary Concepts

### Boundary Integrity

The Chimera's survival depends on maintaining internal boundary rules.

Failure may present as:

- signal pain
- perception splitting
- localized body-temperature anomalies
- equipment interference
- old infrastructure response
- medical data contradiction
- identity-record mismatch
- false negative diagnostics

### Living Edge-State

Analogy for tissue, signal behavior, or identity pattern that exists only at the boundary between incompatible systems.

Story use:

A Chimera may sense systems at boundaries because part of them lives in boundary logic.

Do not make this superpower casual.

### Precise Imbalance

Non-Hermitian / gain-loss analogy.

The Chimera is not balanced because everything is equal.

The Chimera survives because incompatible processes are held in a precise, costly imbalance.

Possible line:

**She was not stable. She was correctly unstable.**

### Exceptional Threshold

A tiny change in stress, signal, medication, terrain, or old infrastructure can produce disproportionate symptoms or effects.

Useful for Ildi-like characters:

A small ghost handshake may matter more to her than to a normal signal reader.

### Correction Drift

Quantum error-correction analogy adapted to identity / signal / biology.

A Chimera's body, records, or signal pattern may continuously correct errors, but correction itself can create drift.

Hydropolis legal implication:

Which correction is the person?
