## Purpose

This file preserves the physics analogy pantry generated by the Analogy Room / Pattern Cartography Lab.

It does **not** canonize mechanisms.

It collects real-science pattern-shapes that may help Meridian Prime develop fictional technologies, myths, corporate distortions, and protected anomalies without turning the setting into physics homework or quantum glitter soup.

Core principle:

**Use real physics as seed crystal, not as cage.**

---

## Locked Canon Constraints

These constraints must remain intact:

- Antimatter / conjugate-life matter-contact must not be made casually safe.
- A Contact Frame does not make antimatter safe. It prevents contact.
- True Àæonos-origin visitors require Conjugate Contact Frames / Mirror-Shells in matter environments.
- Antisapiens are matter-compatible áæbsconditian phenotype people produced by Meridian Prime after first contact with Àæonos.
- Conjugate Chimeras are rare contained bi-phase anomalies, not the Antisapien norm.
- Hydropolis controls legitimate matter-side Àæonos visitation.
- The Dry Port remains protected suspicion.
- The Core and gateway mechanics remain unsolved.
- Chrono-Floquet Distillate remains fictional and story-facing; do not create real-world toxicological procedure.

## Six Recommended Pillars

### 1. Non-Contact Containment

Real-science seed:

- antimatter annihilation
- Penning traps
- magnetic minimum traps
- vacuum isolation
- cryogenic containment
- radiation shielding

Meridian Prime use:

- Contact Frames
- Mirror-Shell docks
- Hydropolis port law
- breach alarms
- framewright guilds
- World Works envy of Hydropolis

Core adaptation:

**Contact Frames are mobile non-contact civilizations in miniature.**

Risk:

Low if it reinforces containment rather than safe contact.

## Quarantined Seeds

The following may be useful as in-world overclaim, myth, failed WWISSaRD theory, or sandbox-only speculation, but should not become practical canon mechanisms without deliberate review:

- Majorana modes
- ER=EPR
- traversable wormholes
- closed timelike curves
- holographic principle as literal solution
- quantum consciousness
- negative energy as unlimited fuel
- generic multiverse explanations
- nanotech solves everything

Recommended use:

**Let World Works / WWISSaRD overclaim these in memos. Let Hydropolis distrust them. Let the Craton translate them into threshold warnings. Do not let them solve the plot.**

## Common Mistakes To Avoid

- Do not use quantum entanglement as instant communication.
- Do not make antimatter touch matter safely without containment.
- Do not make negative energy an unlimited magic fuel.
- Do not use wormholes as casual travel.
- Do not treat time crystals as time machines.
- Do not use consciousness quantum hand-waving.
- Do not let nanotech solve everything.
- Do not make a generic multiverse explanation for every mystery.
- Do not reveal the Dry Port, Core, or gateway mechanics through physics vocabulary.
