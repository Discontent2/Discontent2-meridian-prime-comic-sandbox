## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This document expands Meridian Prime's black market beyond fauna pharmacopoeia into a full shadow economy.

The black market should not merely sell illegal goods.

It should sell:

- survival denied by official systems
- records power tried to erase
- maps that contradict institutional truth
- sacred objects taken where they should not have been taken
- counterfeit safety
- forbidden tools
- stolen proof
- false identity
- dangerous hope

---

## Core Principle

**Candidate Canon Seed**

A black market item on Meridian Prime is not contraband because it is always evil.

It is contraband because someone powerful cannot afford for it to move freely.

Final principle:

**The Meridian Prime black market does not sell evil. It sells what law failed to provide, what power tried to erase, and what greed should never have touched.**

---

## Black Market Philosophies

**Candidate Canon Seeds**

### 1. Survival Black Market

People buy what official systems denied them.

Examples:

- Ghost Fuel
- illegal medicine
- Flood Cache Tokens
- unmetered water
- unfiled repair stamps
- route contraband

Moral tone:

Tragic, practical, morally gray.

### 2. Secret Black Market

People buy what official systems erased.

Examples:

- Echo Receipts
- Lost Kin Lists
- Redline Maps
- Blackbox Teeth
- Bought Nation Birth Ledgers

Moral tone:

Political, dangerous, necessary.

### 3. Desecration Black Market

People buy what should never have been taken.

Examples:

- Weight Theft relics
- sacred fauna material
- Craton No-Map Sketches
- stolen Hydropolis identity objects
- sacred Craton threshold items

Moral tone:

Horror, taboo, consequence.

# 1. Route Contraband

**Development Apocrypha / Candidate Canon Seeds**

Goods that help people survive the Traverse outside official Prime Ops control.

| Item | Description | Buyers | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ghost Fuel** | unregistered fuel stored off-route | Free Scabs, NCI crews, desperate convoys | contaminated batches destroy engines |
| **Deadman Batteries** | salvaged power cells from wrecks | mechanics, scavengers | unstable, traceable, sometimes stolen from graves |
| **False Camp Beacons** | route lights used to fake safe camps | raiders, smugglers | can lure rescue crews into death zones |
| **Dry Circuit Keys** | copied gate or checkpoint credentials | contractors, deserters | Prime Ops hunts these hard |
| **Maw-Weather Charts** | unofficial storm and pressure forecasts | Traverse crews, pirates | more accurate than official maps, politically embarrassing |
| **Unfiled Repair Stamps** | forged repair certification plates | NCI shops, smugglers | can get unsafe vehicles cleared |

Strong candidate:

**Maw-Weather Charts** may be illegal because they are often more truthful than official logistics.

# 2. Cartography Contraband

**Development Apocrypha / Candidate Canon Seeds**

| Item | Description | Buyers | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Redline Maps** | hand-corrected maps showing route deaths and corporate omissions | Reclaimers, NCI, Free Scabs | possession implies distrust of official maps |

| **Under-City Hydropolis Plans** | old dry-season lower-city access diagrams | thieves, spies, historians | Hydropolis considers them identity/security threats |

**Protected Mystery Guardrail**

- Dry Port truth
- Core mystery
- gateway mechanics
- Thunder Egg / Stone Heart truth
- exact Craton sacred interior geography
- Rob's full death-chain implications
- Horus Skelton's full plan
