## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This file defines how Meridian Prime factions interpret, regulate, exploit, condemn, or smuggle fauna-derived black market pharmacopoeia.

The same substance may be:

- folk medicine
- poached contraband
- sacred desecration
- civic-origin violation
- route performance drug
- pirate courage product
- corporate research asset
- desperate survival tool
- counterfeit cure

---

## Core Legal Principle

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Fauna pharmacopoeia law is not only about whether a substance works.

It asks:

- Was the animal harmed?
- Was the material shed, scavenged, or carved?
- Who witnessed the origin?
- What route moved it?
- Whose sacred ecology was violated?
- Who profits from pain?
- Who was denied official care first?

Core line:

**A cure taken from a body still belongs to the wound it made.**

# 1. The Free Scabs

**Development Apocrypha / Political Development**

The Free Scabs are major movers of mixed-quality black market pharmacopoeia.

They sell:

- real medicine
- fake medicine
- stolen medicine
- medicine that works but damns the buyer politically
- medicine that is actually bait
- medicine that is mostly grog wearing a doctor's hat

## The Red Table

**Candidate Canon Seed**

**The Red Table** is a black medical market within or associated with **The Throat** in The Scab Cathedral / Eschar Slough system.

It is where Free Scabs sell, trade, threaten over, or arbitrate:

- battlefield remedies
- fauna derivatives
- emergency sutures
- pain dullers
- pirate courage products
- counterfeit cures
- poached sacred material
- Hydropolis-origin medical contraband
- route-performance substances

Possible Red Table rule:

**At the Red Table, a cure costs less if you do not ask what still had a pulse yesterday.**

## Free Scab Moral Split

**Development Apocrypha**

Free Scabs should not be unified in attitude.

Possible internal divisions:

- **Soft Hands** may reject poached sacred-body products and use only shed / scavenged material.
- **Dust Tithe** may sell the worst derivatives and call conscience a luxury tax.
- **Rust Clerks** may forge origin tags and route permits.
- **Dead Channel Choir** may move Hydropolis-origin contraband through signal-noise corridors.
- **Gate-Broken** may violently reject Craton sacred-body contraband.

Free Scab contradiction:

They may save a dying child with illegal medicine and sell poisoned hope to the next desperate adult.

# 2. The Anticlinal Craton

**Religious Tradition / Political Development**

The Craton views some fauna-derived contraband as more than poaching.

It can be sacred theft from pressure ecology, threshold life, and guest-law memory.

## Weight Theft

**Candidate Canon Seed**

**Weight Theft** is the Craton term for sacred fauna contraband.

It means stealing not merely tissue, but the animal's place in pressure, route, memory, and sacred land.

Examples may include:

- Thunderback Resonance Marrow
- unauthorized Geode Crown Raptor nest material
- pressure-corridor fauna derivatives
- sacred Craton beast products

Craton principle:

**Some contraband is not illegal because it is dangerous. It is dangerous because it was stolen from a sacred animal and then named medicine.**

## Likely Craton Response

**Religious Tradition**

For ordinary smuggling, there may be negotiation.

For Weight Theft, response may include:

- permanent denial of threshold
- refusal of guest-right
- seizure or ritual destruction of material
- pursuit of poachers
- political rupture with whoever purchased or protected the trade
- closure of wet-gate hospitality to implicated parties

Do not define exact punishments yet.

# 3. Hydropolis

**Political Development / Scientific Hypothesis**

Hydropolis cares about origin verification.

Fauna-derived medicine without documented origin is suspect even if it works.

## Unwitnessed Biological Therapeutic

**Candidate Canon Seed / Corporate Distortion Adjacent**

Hydropolis term:

**Unwitnessed Biological Therapeutic.**

Translation:

**Maybe medicine. Maybe crime. Maybe identity contamination.**

Hydropolis concerns include:

- verified origin
- ecological protection
- signal exposure
- civic identity continuity
- archive contamination
- wet-zone protected fauna
- counterfeit medicines from unverified animal material

Key substances:

- Glasswake Ray Cartilage Jelly
- Mooring Leviathan Resonance Oil
- tower-associated fauna products
- unverified wet-zone animal derivatives

Hydropolis principle:

**A medicine without origin is an injury with better manners.**

# 4. Prime Ops

**Political Development / Corporate Distortion**

Prime Ops treats fauna pharmacopoeia as a route safety, liability, contamination, and black-market medical problem.

## Possible Prime Ops Terms

- **Unverified Faunal Medical Product**
- **Prohibited Caustic Faunal Derivative**
- **Operator Impairment Risk**
- **Biological Route Contamination Item**
- **Unauthorized Trauma Material**

Prime Ops behavior:

- confiscates black-market fauna products
- catalogs origin when possible
- bans route-performance derivatives before dangerous operations
- quietly uses illegal material when official kits run dry
- punishes some users while depending on the same underground supply chain during emergencies

Prime Ops contradiction:

They confiscate the jar, write the report, and sometimes open the jar anyway.

# 5. World Works

**Corporate Distortion**

World Works sees profit, research value, workforce preservation, patent opportunity, and liability management.

## Possible World Works Term

**Nonstandard Biological Performance Asset.**

Translation:

**We want to own the patent before anyone calls it poaching.**

World Works may:

- sponsor deniable research
- launder poached material through subsidiaries
- relabel local medicine as proprietary therapy
- suppress injury reports tied to black products
- criminalize worker possession while studying corporate samples
- treat sacred-body contraband as biomedical opportunity
- patent a shed-harvest tradition and call it innovation

Do not make World Works cartoonishly evil.

Make them bureaucratically hungry.

# 6. The Bought Nation

**Political Development**

Bought Nation communities are split.

Some preserve ethical shed-harvest traditions.

Others buy black market cures because official medicine arrives too late, too expensive, or not at all.

## Core Moral Rule

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Bought Nation saying:

**Shed is gift. Carved is debt. Poached is curse.**

This may be the moral backbone of the fauna pharmacopoeia.

### Tolerated / Folk Use

- shed Glassback Plate Dust
- shed Shardhorn Wader Horn-Shavings
- wallow-gathered Basin Roller Mud-Fat
- non-live Mudvault Shell-Lacquer
- shed-certified Cut Strider Tendon Thread

### Condemned

- calf-derived Plate Dust
- rookery-stolen Rime Condor Lung-Salts
- poached Strider Thread
- Gulper Bile sold as medicine
- Craton sacred-body material
- contaminated black clinic products

Bought Nation conflict:

When law fails to heal workers, black medicine becomes both crime and testimony.

# 7. NCI / Traverse Crews

**Development Apocrypha**

NCI and Traverse crews are pragmatic but not foolish.

They distinguish:

- material that works
- material that works only if sourced ethically
- material that is fraud
- material that works but causes worse problems later
- material that should never have entered a field kit

NCI likely sayings:

- **No. Absolutely not. Put that jar back.**
- **If the cure smells like a predator, ask who dragged it here.**
- **Shed tag or trash fire. Pick one.**

NCI may keep unofficial field notes that are more accurate than Prime Ops reports and less legal than anyone prefers.

# 9. Product-Class Law

**Development Apocrypha**

## Desperation Medicine

Treated with sympathy by communities and suspicion by authorities.

Examples:

- Glassback Plate Dust
- Cut Strider Tendon Thread
- Basin Roller Mud-Fat
- Glasswake Ray Cartilage Jelly
- Mudvault Shell-Lacquer

Legal tension:

The law may punish possession of the thing that saved a life.

## Route Performance Drugs

Often punished harshly because impairment can kill whole convoys.

Examples:

- Rime Condor Lung-Salts
- Cataract Eel Shock-Gel
- Geode Crown Raptor Crest-Powder

Legal tension:

Crews use them when schedules are impossible.

## Pirate and Raider Pharmacopoeia

Treated as criminal and tactical.

Examples:

- Blackwake Croc Jaw-Salts
- Reef-Hammer Drake Tooth Oil
- Slough Humpback Filter-Milk
- Paleochannel Gulper Bile / Mouth-Gold

Legal tension:

Some substances are medicine in a shipwreck and contraband at a checkpoint.

## Sacred Body Contraband

Treated as desecration, not mere drug crime.

Examples:

- Thunderback Resonance Marrow
- sacred Craton fauna material
- unauthorized pressure-corridor derivatives

Legal tension:

Outsider courts may not understand the scale of insult.

## Civic-Origin Violations

Hydropolis-specific and identity-adjacent.

Examples:

- Glasswake Ray Cartilage Jelly
- Mooring Leviathan Resonance Oil
- unverified wet-zone animal derivatives

Legal tension:

A working medicine may still be an unwitnessed civic crime.
