## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This document defines how different Meridian Prime authorities classify, tolerate, ban, bless, exploit, smuggle, or misunderstand intoxicants, medicines, ritual foods, fermented beverages, and dangerous foodlike substances.

The same substance may be:

- medicine in one jurisdiction
- contraband in another
- sacrament in a third
- ration in a fourth
- poison in the hands of a fool
- corporate liability on paper

---

## Core Legal Principle

**Candidate Canon Seed**

Substance law on Meridian Prime is not primarily about chemistry.

It is about trust, origin, jurisdiction, route risk, labor control, civic identity, guest law, and who gets to name danger.

Core line:

**A cup becomes legal when the right authority survives naming it.**

## Broad Classification System

**Development Apocrypha**

Possible cross-faction categories:

| Class | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Ration-Safe** | accepted as ordinary food or route support within verified context |
| **Medicinal-Restricted** | permitted only by trained local experts, medics, civic offices, or ritual authorities |
| **Ceremonial-Controlled** | legal or proper only inside ritual, guest law, festival, or civic sequence |
| **Route-Prohibited** | banned before or during dangerous travel, docking, descent, or convoy duty |
| **Civic-Quarantined** | Hydropolis-style record, identity, signal, or archive contamination risk |
| **Sacred / Guest-Law Restricted** | Craton foods or medicines given only after proper threshold status |
| **Contraband / Smuggled Comfort** | traded illegally across faction boundaries |
| **Toxic Lookalike Hazard** | foodlike substance dangerous due to misidentification |
| **Criminal Use Marker** | associated with sabotage, sedation, coercion, or ambush lore |
| **Do Not Name Publicly** | taboo or politically suppressed category |

# 1. Bought Nation Treatment

**Political Development**

The Bought Nation treats foods, drinks, medicines, and intoxicants through labor memory, survival rights, kin knowledge, and suspicion of corporate labels.

### Common / Tolerated

- Fog Pear Cider
- Cloud Morel Brown Ale
- Shardwake Chew
- Dust Lentil Blackcake
- Cloud Morel Stew-Tea
- Mossroot survival foods
- Rimebutton Tea in highland settlements

### Restricted / Taboo

- Mossblind
- Brideglow
- Bellpod Sleep
- Hollow Truth
- White Sleep Mercy
- unauthorized Blue Vein Still

### Legal Culture

Bought Nation communities may trust:

- route aunties
- field medics
- NCI elders
- harvest apprenticeships
- Reclaimer food councils
- Charterhold market reputation

They may distrust:

- World Works safety labels
- Prime Ops blanket bans
- outsider harvesters
- foods whose local names have been replaced by corporate codes

Bought Nation saying:

**Drink what your aunt can name. Spit out what the company labeled.**

# 6. Hydropolis Treatment

**Political Development / Scientific Hypothesis**

Hydropolis regulates substances through verified origin, civic identity, signal integrity, archive risk, and wet-season public safety.

### Common / Legal With Verification

- Tower Gill Infusion
- Mirror Cap broths
- Moor-Nut Milkbeer
- Glasswake Plum Wine
- Signal Ribbon Broth
- verified Signal Kelp Ribbons
- civic-cleared Moor-Lily products

### Restricted / Illegal

- Drift
- Drift Gills
- Black Signal
- Black Signal Berries
- Signal Nuts
- Archive Buttons
- Archive Grapes
- contaminated Tower Gill Sprouts

### Hydropolis Legal Principle

**A pleasure that breaks the record is not private.**

### Civic Logic

Hydropolis does not merely ask:

**Is this food poisonous?**

It asks:

- Where did it grow?
- What signal field touched it?
- What record did it pass through?
- Did it compromise identity continuity?
- Was its harvest witnessed?

Hydropolis phrase:

**Food without verified origin is a record risk.**
