## Purpose

**Development Apocrypha**

This file captures the first-pass pharmacopeia layer for Meridian Prime.

It covers fictional:

- teas and tisanes
- stimulant foods
- depressants and sleep agents
- fermented beverages
- narcotics and illegal comforts
- hallucinogens and vision foods
- ceremonial foods and drinks
- medicinal preparations
- recreational use patterns

This is a story-facing cultural layer, not a real-world guide.

---

## Safety / Story Guardrail

**Do Not Promote**

All substance effects, medical uses, and intoxicant categories in this file are fictional.

Do not provide exact recipes, preparation methods, dosages, extraction processes, or real-world analogues.

This material should support culture, law, ecology, ritual, and plot, not practical substance use.

# 9. Recreational Use and Vice by Civilization

## Bought Nation

**Political Development**

Recreation is political, communal, and labor-tired.

Common:

- Fog Pear Cider
- Cloud Morel Ale
- Shardwake Chew
- Dust Lentil Blackcake at night-shift tables

Taboo:

- Mossblind
- Bellpod Sleep
- Bridecap Glow

Bought Nation saying:

**Drink what your aunt can name. Spit out what the company labeled.**

## Hydropolis

**Political Development / Scientific Hypothesis**

Recreation is regulated by identity, signal, and verified origin.

Common:

- Moor-Nut Milkbeer
- Glasswake Plum Wine
- Tower Gill dishes
- Mirror Cap broths

Underground:

- Drift
- Black Signal
- Drift Gills
- Signal Nuts

Hydropolis warning:

**A pleasure that breaks the record is not private.**

## Craton

**Religious Tradition**

Recreation and ceremony are hard to separate.

Permitted only by role, status, threshold, or guest relationship:

- Pressure Nuts
- Geode Milk Broth
- Maw Bean meals
- Spray Grape Dockwine

Taboo or outsider rumor:

- Gateblind Vision
- Black Tongue Stillness
- Thunder Greens ordeal rumors

Craton principle:

**Do not eat what has not received you.**

## Free Scabs

**Development Apocrypha / Political Development**

Recreation is survival wearing a pirate hat.

Common:

- Blackwater Fig Grog
- Blackcap Smoke Broth
- Pirate Bean stew
- Scab Yam mash

Dark contraband:

- Brideglow
- Tar Sleep
- False Harbor Fruit
- Procession Light

Free Scab rule:

**Never trust a glowing fruit, a clean cup, or a sober pirate selling both.**

**Protected Mystery Guardrail**

Intoxicants, medicines, and ritual foods may deepen daily life, law, vice, medicine, ritual, and faction identity.

They must not casually reveal:

- Dry Port truth
- Core mystery
- gateway mechanics
- Thunder Egg / Stone Heart truth
- exact Craton sacred interior geography
- Rob's full death-chain implications
- Horus Skelton's full plan
- whether Archive Mold preserves literal memory

These substances can carry rumor, taboo, and partial evidence without solving protected mysteries.
