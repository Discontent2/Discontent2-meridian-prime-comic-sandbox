# PAGE 23

**Page Role:** Inside Back Cover / In-World Warning Ad

**Adaptation Label:** Adaptation Choice / Candidate Canon for poster language

**Panel Count:** No traditional panels. Poster / ad page.

**Page Purpose:** Let the reader breathe after Page 22 while reinforcing Traverse culture. This page should function as both a comic back-matter ad framework and an in-world warning poster.

## Poster Layout

**Visual:** Full-page in-world NCI / Traverse warning poster. Weathered field-paper texture over full-bleed industrial cold. A simplified silhouette of Red Umbrielor and a Mod train crosses a black-flagged route in the lower third. The upper field is open enough for big typography. Background may include faint packet fragments, route markers, old approval blocks, and a ghost of the Page 13 signal motif.

**Primary Poster Text:**

THINGS ARE DIFFERENT ON TRAVERSE.

**Secondary Poster Text:**

NCI accepts private route contracts, field repairs, survey support, and bad decisions with paperwork.

**Small In-World Text:**

Ask your Lead before trusting the map.  
Ask your mechanic before trusting the machine.  
Ask your signal tech before trusting the silence.

**Required Production Note:**

Keep text/logos inside the safe area (0.25 in from trim). Backgrounds may extend to bleed.

**Optional Footer:**

NOCSID CONTRACTORS INC.  
THE FAMILY / THE COMPANY / FIELD WORK DONE UGLY

**Lettering Note:** The required production note must remain readable and inside the safe area. Poster headline can be large and stark. Background graphics may extend to bleed.

**Reveal Control:** No new story reveal. This is a culture echo, not an extra plot page.

# PAGE 24

**Page Role:** Back Cover / Back-Story Image Page

**Adaptation Label:** Non-Canon Promo / Adaptation Art

**Panel Count:** 1 full-bleed back-cover image

**Page Purpose:** End where the issue began: The House Ten Built in the cold, one light still burning. Close on Rob's agency, not plot explanation.

## Back Cover Image

**Visual:** Full-bleed image of The House Ten Built, tiny against Meridian Prime cold. The landscape is vast, lethal, and quiet. One warm light burns in the cabin window. A faint suggestion of old tracks leads away from the house, almost lost under new frost. The composition should echo Page 3 but feel more mythic and final.

No figures need to be visible. If any silhouette appears, keep it ambiguous and non-literal: a memory of Rob near the window, a hint of Horus outside, or Red Umbrielor's track-memory fading into the route. Avoid making it a ghost scene.

**Caption / Teaser Text:**

Rob did not die because he lost.

He died because he chose.

**Optional Footer Text:**

MERIDIAN PRIME WILL RETURN

**Lettering Note:** Caption, title/logo, website/socials, and any return text must stay inside the safe area. The landscape and cabin image may extend through bleed.

**Reveal Control:** May imply Rob's final agency. Do not reveal the House's full secrets, the exact route destination, or the full pistol function.
