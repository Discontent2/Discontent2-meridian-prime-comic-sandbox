## Purpose

Page 07 is the **Lindy threshold / packet handoff page**.

It is not the Page 06 empty-seat grief page.

- Lindy Nocsid crossing the NCI staging yard with packet materials

## Recommended Panel Breakdown

### Panel 1

Medium-wide exterior panel.

Lindy Nocsid crosses the NCI staging yard with a dispatch slate tucked under one arm and a hardcopy packet under the other. She moves like irritation learned to wear boots. Field crews part without making a ceremony of it.

Required details:

- NCI staging yard before dawn
- Red Umbrielor nearby or implied as destination
- field lights, cold vapor, patched machinery, yard grit
- Lindy with dispatch slate and hardcopy packet
- Lindy in practical field/dispatch wear
- cap recommended for working-default Lindy
- no full crew lineup
- no scenic polar route

Approved captions:

```text
Lindy Nocsid did not wave.
```

```text
Lindy considered waving an admission that people were far away from each other.
```

Tone:

Dry, sharp, already tired of the morning.

---

### Panel 2

Medium cab-doorway panel.

Lindy climbs the side step and leans into Red Umbrielor's cab doorway. Thermal cap low, sharp eyes, small frame carrying more authority than her job title admits.

Required details:

- Lindy on the threshold, not fully inside
- Red Umbrielor cab interior visible behind / around Tenet
- Tenet inside or turning toward her
- cold exterior behind Lindy
- warmer cab interior behind Tenet
- threshold posture should carry emotional boundary

Approved dialogue:

```text
Lindy: Your defrost still dead?
```

```text
Tenet: Resting.
```

```text
Lindy: Runs in the family.
```

Text rule:

Three short balloons. Avoid crowding the doorway or covering Lindy's eyes.

---

### Panel 3

Small or medium interior comedy beat.

Lindy sniffs the cab air with theatrical suspicion. The turkey roaster glows behind Tenet.

Required details:

- turkey roaster continuity from Page 06
- dull orange heater glow in bolted cage
- Lindy suspicious of the cab smell / heater setup
- Tenet dry, not embarrassed
- the joke should not swallow the page

Approved dialogue:

```text

```

```text

```

```text

```

Tone:

Sibling banter, machine culture, grief hiding in jokes.

### Panel 4

Larger handoff panel.

Lindy shoves the packet at Tenet. The hardcopy is in a field sleeve, stiff with cold. At the same time, the digital twin blinks onto Red Umbrielor's dash.

Required details:

- physical packet in Lindy's hand
- Tenet receiving or reaching for it
- digital twin appearing on Red Umbrielor's dash
- cold field sleeve / stiff paperwork
- screen glow / CHIME moment
- packet text readable enough to establish MITE II but not reveal Page 08

### Panel 5

Quiet final panel.

Lindy braces in the doorway, not fully entering. She has delivered the packet, but not surrendered her distance. Tenet starts opening the packet; Lindy watches his face instead of the screen.

Required details:

- Lindy still in threshold posture
- Tenet opening / beginning to read packet
- screen or paper glow may be visible but not dominant
- Lindy's attention should be on Tenet's reaction
- dread runway into Page 08

Approved dialogue:

```text
Lindy: Good morning to you too.
```

```text
Tenet: It was morning before you opened the door.
```

Tone:

Banter continues, but the packet has weight. The final image should make the reader want the page turn.

## Common Failure Modes

These are likely image-generation failure modes for Page 07 and should be actively avoided:

1. **Page 06 grief drift**  
   The generator re-centers the empty passenger seat, under-seat compartment, or pistol.

2. **Page 08 stamp drift**  
   The generator reveals Horus's approval too early.

3. **Crew drift**  
   The generator introduces Rudy, Forest, Ildi, Rob, or the full team.

4. **Exterior drift**  
   The generator turns the page into a Red Umbrielor yard splash instead of threshold/cab storytelling.

5. **Packet over-detail drift**  
   The generator fills the page with fake bureaucracy and unreadable documents.

6. **Polar drift**  
   The exterior yard becomes an Antarctic snowfield.

7. **Generic sibling drift**  
   Lindy becomes soft, sweet, neutral, or decorative instead of sharp, bruised, funny when cornered, and too awake for the hour.

8. **Turkey-roaster gag drift**  
   The joke swallows the page instead of supporting the packet handoff.

Treat these as hard failures.
