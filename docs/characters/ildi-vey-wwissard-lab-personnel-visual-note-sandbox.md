## Purpose

This file clarifies how to depict WWISSaRD / WWISSaRD-adjacent personnel in Ildi Vey's Conjugate Chimera creation-horror backstory.

## Core Visual Rule

WWISSaRD should not be visually treated as a human-only institution or a non-human-only institution.

The best default is:

> WWISSaRD is human-majority by institutional origin, but not human-only by mandate.

Because WWISSaRD comes from a Meridian Prime human faction / institutional network, most scientists, administrators, guards, and technicians will naturally be human. However, the institution may employ, contract, coerce, consult, or contain non-human specialists when their expertise is useful.

## Ildi Experiment Scene Guidance

In scenes involving Ildi's Bi-Phase Integration experiment, the lab staff may include:

- mostly Meridian Prime human scientists, administrators, security, and medical staff;
- one or more Antisapien signal-band specialists or technicians;
- Aeonolacertian observers or consultants, especially when biofluorescent scar-state perception matters;
- non-human contractors, deniable consultants, or coerced experts;
- silhouette-only figures whose species is deliberately unclear.

Do not imply that every WWISSaRD scientist is Aeonolacertian or Antisapien just because non-human specialists appear in a scene.

Do not imply that every WWISSaRD scientist must be human either.

## Why This Matters For Ildi

Ildi's creation-horror story is about institutional exploitation, not species destiny.

The horror should come from the fact that a human-majority institution can draw in many kinds of people through funding, fear, ambition, coercion, expertise, debt, or survival pressure.

Mixed personnel can sharpen the scene if used carefully:

- human administrators control the forms;
- human-majority staff preserve the factional identity of WWISSaRD;
- an Antisapien specialist may understand the signal-band implications too well;
- an Aeonolacertian observer may see scar biofluorescence unaided and become a vital witness or complicit instrument;
- silhouettes can preserve ambiguity around who knew what.

## Friend Portrayal Rule

If Ildi's betraying friend appears in visual development for the experiment scene, they should usually appear as a silhouette, reflection, obscured figure, old footage fragment, shadow behind glass, or incomplete memory shape.

The friend should feel intimate and present without becoming visually overdefined too early.

## Final Visual Principle

**WWISSaRD's Ildi lab should feel human-majority, institutionally cold, and politically wide-reaching, with non-human personnel used as specific story pressure rather than default monster imagery.**
