# Cross-Room Handoffs

> Sandbox guide for passing usable material between Meridian Prime rooms.

**Sandbox Status:** Workflow Guide  
**Canon Status:** Not canon. Handoffs translate canon and sandbox work for another room. They do not replace source-of-truth files.  
**Authoritative Promotion Rules:** `Discontent2/meridian-prime:docs/development/sandbox-promotion-rules.md`

## Purpose

Handoffs help one room prepare material for another room without forcing the next room to rediscover everything.

Examples:

- Story to Comic
- Worldbuilding to Visual Development
- Canon to Toy Development
- Analogy Room to Worldbuilding
- Story to Game / Tabletop

## What Handoffs Do

A handoff should:

- identify source files used
- separate canon from sandbox material
- flag protected mysteries
- translate material into the receiving room's needs
- list useful opportunities
- list what not to reveal or overdefine
- suggest next outputs

## What Handoffs Do Not Do

A handoff does not:

- promote lore to canon
- replace canon files
- duplicate entire source files
- solve protected mysteries
- become a master bible
- force every room to use the same format

## Handoff Template

```md
# [Source Room] to [Receiving Room] Handoff

**Sandbox Status:** Handoff Note  
**Canon Status:** Not canon. Use cited source files for authority.  
**Source Room:**  
**Receiving Room:**  
**Date:**  

## Purpose

Short explanation of what this handoff prepares.

## Source Files Used

- `path/to/source-file.md`

## Canon Material

Short bullet list of confirmed material the receiving room can rely on.

## Sandbox / Development Material

Short bullet list of experimental material and its current label.

## Protected Mysteries

List mysteries touched and what must not be revealed.

## Opportunities for Receiving Room

- Visual opportunity
- Story opportunity
- Toy opportunity
- Game opportunity
- Comic opportunity

## Do Not Do

- Do not solve protected mysteries.
- Do not promote sandbox material by accident.
- Do not copy whole canon files.

## Recommended Next Output

One clear next deliverable.
```

## Core Rule

Handoffs are bridges, not new capitals.

The sandbox should remain wild. Promotion should be disciplined.
