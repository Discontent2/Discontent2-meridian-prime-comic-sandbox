# Rejected / Do Not Promote

**Sandbox Status:** Development Department Control Log  
**Label:** Rejected / Do Not Promote  
**Canon Boundary:** This file tracks useful experiments that should remain outside main canon.

## Purpose

Not every useful sandbox idea should become canon.

This file preserves rejected, risky, contradictory, overly explanatory, or format-specific experiments so the Development Department can learn from them without accidentally promoting them.

## Rejection Categories

### Too Central

The idea makes a side story secretly responsible for main canon events.

### Too Explanatory

The idea solves a mystery that should remain protected.

### Too Generic

The idea could belong to any sci-fi universe and does not feel Meridian Prime-specific.

### Too Contradictory

The idea breaks existing canon, species logic, Traverse terminology, visual logic, or reveal order.

### Too Promotional

The idea works as trailer, poster, cover, or hype imagery but should not be treated as literal continuity.

### Too Cute

The idea is funny but weakens tone, procedure, survival pressure, or character seriousness.

### Useful but Non-Canon

The idea remains valuable as sandbox material, adaptation compression, What-If Continuity, or format experiment.

## Current Do-Not-Promote Warnings

These are broad warning zones, not rejected pitches.

### Side Character Secretly Caused Main Plot

Do not make a sandbox protagonist secretly responsible for Rob's poisoning, MITE II, Horus's plan, the hidden formation, or The House Ten Built.

### Every Myth Is Literally True

Meridian Prime benefits from rumor, field memory, and ambiguity. Do not literalize every ghost story.

### Cosmic Explanation Creep

Do not let a side story about fuel, flags, signals, or equipment become the explanation for the Core or gateway mechanics.

### Generic Monster Episode

Do not replace Meridian Prime's procedural danger with ordinary monster-of-the-week logic unless the story is clearly a What-If or format experiment.

### Overpowered New Faction

Small factions, local crews, and isolated cultures are welcome. Avoid inventing a new group that invalidates World Works, Prime Ops, NCI, Aeonolacertian, or Absconditian power structures.

### Main Character Cameos as Solutions

Avoid having Tenet, Rob, Lindy, Horus, or Red Umbrielor appear just to solve a side-story problem.

## Rejection Philosophy

A rejected idea is not trash. It is a marked hazard.

Sometimes the warning flag is the most useful thing the route leaves behind.
