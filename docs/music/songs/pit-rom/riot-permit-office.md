## Core Concept

```text
The permit office does not prevent riots. It pre-approves the damage.
```

Track 2 is designed to split away from Track 1. Where `Condemned Block Intro` uses downward grime and bass descent, `Riot Permit Office` uses muted command violence, stop-start assault, lower FM lead movement, and tribunal-engine pressure.
