## Core Style

Wet Signal District makes rainfield ambient electronic music: slow, immersive, neon-wet, melodic, and cinematic without becoming polished synthwave.

It is a Meridian Prime love letter to rainlit ambient electronic traditions, translated through Pulse Width Music's hardware-bound, signal-damaged language.

Core phrase:

```text
Wet Signal District makes electronic music for cities that only exist after the rain has already remembered you.
```
