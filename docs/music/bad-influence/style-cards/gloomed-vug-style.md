## Core Identity

Gloomed Vug is the first young Aeonolacertian duo signed to Arrestthemall Records.

They sound less forensic than Dead Mile Cypher and more like a bad-room myth recorded on a dying phone beside a grave mound. Their music should feel raw, blown-out, melancholic, hostile, and weirdly intimate.

Tumulus makes the grave knock.

Shade Masiff makes the grave answer.

## Lyric Formatting Rules

Gloomed Vug lyrics should feel younger, rawer, and less forensic than Dead Mile Cypher.

Use:

- short repeated hooks
- grave-bell images
- cave / mineral / mud language
- species-class alienation
- clutch shame
- cloak imagery
- night-road dread
- distorted ad-libs
- simple slogans that feel cursed rather than polished
- Tumulus low-end resentment
- Shade Masiff mournful distance

Avoid:

- Dead Mile Cypher-style legal argument complexity
- clean political exposition
- mature testimonial clarity from Shame
- Blue Tapetum philosophical speed-rap
- Burn Notice corporate-invoice satire as the main voice
- Discontent One field-tape architecture as the main voice
