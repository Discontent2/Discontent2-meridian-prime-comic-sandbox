## Core Identity

Dead Mile Cipher is Meridian Prime’s underground backpack rap collective.

Their sound blends:

- abstract indie-rap experimentation
- emotionally direct underground rap
- mythic crew architecture
- dusty boom-bap drums
- chopped field recordings
- pirate-radio fragments
- corporate paperwork satire
- false shelter rumors
- Low Road oral history
- factional argument

They should feel like a crew trading verses inside an illegal archive van while the route map changes under the tires.

## Lyric Formatting Rules

Dead Mile Cipher lyrics should be readable as rap verses first.

Use:

- compact 12 to 16 line verses
- internal rhyme clusters
- concrete factional images
- one chantable hook
- member-labeled verses when useful
- short interlude samples from fictional route-radio, invoices, or service tones
- direct emotional line after abstract bars so the listener has a handrail

Avoid:

- overexplaining lore
- turning every line into exposition
- using real-world artist names
- citations inside lyrics
- long sung choruses unless the user requests a crossover
