## Core Identity

Shade Masiff is the cloaked MC / producer of Gloomed Vug.

He is a young adult Bajadrasapien Aeonolacertian whose presence feels like a walking ridge line: tall, mournful, distant, and half-hidden by fabric, fog, and night-road myth.

He is the spectral counterweight to Tumulus. Where Tumulus makes the grave knock, Shade Masiff makes the grave answer.

---

## Species / Anatomy Lock

Shade Masiff is not a human with reptile traits.

He is a fully saurian person with Bajadrasapien traits: saurian head and snout, clawed hands and feet, long balancing tail, intelligent tool-user posture, and a distinctive ridge / sail / spine silhouette that shapes how his cloak hangs.

The cloak should not erase his species. It should reveal his nonhuman outline through shape, tension, shadow, and movement.

---

## Public Persona

Shade Masiff wears a cloak.

The cloak is central to the image lock: not wizard robe, not superhero cape, not fantasy priest costume. It should feel like weatherproof streetwear, night-road concealment, ridge-saurian ritual garment, and underground performance silhouette all at once.

Visual cues:

- long dark cloak shaped by Bajadrasapien ridge anatomy
- hooded or shadowed saurian head, but not faceless
- narrow visible snout line or eye glint
- clawed hands near sampler, recorder, or microphone
- fabric snagged with tags, mineral dust, old route thread, or bone-gray stitching
- portable recorder, small synth, or beat machine partly hidden under cloth
- tall stillness behind Tumulus
- canyon-night color world: mud black, mineral purple, oxidized green, grave-lamp yellow

---

## Emotional Engine

Shade Masiff is distance turned into music.

He does not rush to explain himself. He lets fog do some of the talking.

His wound is not clutch-shame like Tumulus. His wound is distance: being seen as an omen, a silhouette, a strange shape on a ridge, never as a person standing in the weather.

His music insists that shadow can be testimony.

---

## MC Voice

Shade Masiff raps and sings with a thin, echoing, mournful delivery.

He can carry hooks, but they should feel spectral rather than pop-polished. His voice is half-chant, half-murmur, sometimes layered until it feels like ridge wind moving through a cave mouth.

Voice traits:

- thin mournful saurian vocal
- echoing melodic hooks
- cloaked whisper-rap
- distant doubled lines
- fogged harmonies
- slow cadence
- spectral hook fragments
- not clean pop singing
- not theatrical fantasy chanting

---

## Production Style

Shade Masiff produces atmosphere, hooks, and spectral melody.

Core production traits:

- wind drones
- desert-night pads
- detuned plucked-string fragments
- choir-like breath layers
- shrill ridge whistles
- distant bells
- reversed chants
- cloak-rustle percussion
- cavern reverb
- mineral-cave ambience
- ghostly melodic loops
- fogged low-end

He hides melody inside weather.
