## Core Identity

Tumulus is the MC / producer core of Gloomed Vug.

He is a young adult Ankylosapien Aeonolacertian and the runt of his clutch. That wound shapes everything: his posture, his voice, his production, his anger, and his need to make music that sounds heavier than anyone expected him to be.

He was born from a heavy people and built smaller than expected.

He turns mass into sound instead of body.

---

## Species / Anatomy Lock

Tumulus is not a human with reptile traits.

He is a fully saurian person with Ankylosapien traits: armored body language, low center of gravity, heavy tail presence, blunt protective silhouette, clawed hands and feet, saurian head and snout, and upright tool-user posture.

Because he is the runt of his clutch, his body should feel compact and slightly undersized for an Ankylosapien, but never weak, cute, or comedic. He carries a dense, guarded force.

---

## Public Persona

Tumulus looks like a small armored storm trying to stand taller than its own shadow.

Visual cues:

- oversized jacket or hoodie that makes him look even smaller
- heavy boots or wrapped clawed feet
- patched gear bag or sampler case
- grave-bell charm, bone-chain detail, or cracked shield symbol
- mud-black and bone-gray clothing
- compact Ankylosapien armor plates visible around neck, shoulders, arms, and tail base
- scuffed portable sampler / beat machine
- low, guarded stance

He should never look like a fantasy warrior or mascot. He is a young underground producer with saurian anatomy and a bad-room mythology.

---

## Emotional Engine

Tumulus carries clutch-shame.

He was not the biggest, strongest, or most physically impressive member of his clutch. He learned early that every room had already measured him.

His art is his answer.

He does not beg to be seen as heavy. He makes the speakers prove it.

---

## MC Voice

Tumulus raps with a raspy, nasal, half-snarled, half-mumbled delivery.

He often sounds like he is pushing words through dirt and teeth. The vocal should feel close, overdriven, doubled, and occasionally buried under distortion.

Voice traits:

- raspy young saurian snarl
- nasal pressure
- muttered hooks
- half-sung grave melodies
- distorted doubled vocals
- angry but wounded phrasing
- short repeated phrases
- raw underground delivery
- not polished, not clean, not technically pristine

---

## Production Style

Tumulus produces like someone compensating through weight.

Core production traits:

- blown-out bass
- distorted lo-fi trap drums
- slow stomping patterns
- cemetery-bell melodies
- ugly synth choirs
- grave-mound ambience
- armored tail-thud kicks
- muddy kick distortion
- bone-rattle percussion
- overdriven low-end
- cracked speaker texture
- haunted youth energy

His beats should feel too heavy for the room that made them.
