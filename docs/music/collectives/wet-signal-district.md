## Canon / Sandbox Note

Wet Signal District is a working sandbox music identity. This file does not canonize the group, albums, songs, label relationships, or lore references until explicitly promoted.

The project is usable immediately as Pulse Width Music's first non-Enum signing and as a Meridian Prime love letter to rainlit ambient electronic music traditions.

---

## Core Identity

Wet Signal District is Pulse Width Music's dream wing.

Where Enum Rage Engine makes cartridge violence through broken sound chips, Wet Signal District makes long-form electronic music for neon rain, empty platforms, late-night shelter glass, and cities that feel half-remembered by their own transit systems.

Core phrase:

```text
Wet Signal District makes electronic music for cities that only exist after the rain has already remembered you.
```

---

## Influence Translation Rule

Wet Signal District is a Meridian Prime love letter to rainlit ambient electronic music, vapor-adjacent dream music, neon transit ambience, original synth soundscapes, and cinematic post-city melancholy.

It must not become direct imitation.

```text

original synth instrumentation
long emotional pads

distant station chords
```

