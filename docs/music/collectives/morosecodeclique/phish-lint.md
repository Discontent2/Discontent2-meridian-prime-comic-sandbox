## Core Identity

Phish Lint is the producer of morosecodeclique.

He turns blue-lit heartbreak into damaged beat architecture: cold synthetic guitars, broken ringtone melodies, voicemail chops, notification percussion, overcompressed drums, and dead-chat ambience.

Phish Lint makes beats out of things you should not have clicked.

## Public Persona

Phish Lint is ugly-funny, cyber-sleazy, and emotionally precise.

He looks like someone who has three cracked laptops, one working cable, and a beat that hurts too much for how cheap it sounds.

Visual cues:

- stickered laptop
- old headphones or dead earbuds
- black hoodie with lint, pins, and cheap patches
- glowing phone screen lighting blue skin from below
- teal tapetum eye flare in low light
- small beat pad, tiny keyboard, or handheld recorder
- messy bedroom floor, cables, wrappers, old flyers
- screen glow and blacklight dust

---

## Emotional Engine

Phish Lint treats production as emotional residue.

He does not polish sadness. He compresses it until it becomes catchy, brittle, and slightly wrong.

His beats should feel like a corrupted love note, a dead link, or a voice memo bounced too many times.

---
