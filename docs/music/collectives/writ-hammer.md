## Core Purpose

`Writ Hammer` is the in-universe heavy band whose riff logic, slogans, aggression, and visual identity inspire `Red Warrant` BGM.

They are not the primary game-score artist. Their role is to provide source mythology and riff language that can be translated into 16-bit cartridge BGM by `Pit ROM`.

## Name Logic

`Writ` supplies legal/civic violence: warrants, judgments, filings, orders, and official harm.

`Hammer` supplies impact: hardcore downbeats, blunt riffs, and physical force.

Together:

```text
law as weapon
riff as blunt instrument
civic language turned into pit music
```

## Relationship to Red Warrant

`Writ Hammer` is the heavy-band shadow behind the game. Their energy shapes the soundtrack, but the game score itself should normally be generated under `Pit ROM`.

Pipeline:

```text
Writ Hammer writes the riot.
Pit ROM loads it into the cartridge.
Devilment Studio turns it into stages.
```

---

## Relationship to Pit ROM

`Pit ROM` should treat Writ Hammer's songs as raw material for BGM translation.

Translation rules:

```text
full-band riff becomes square-wave riff bass
vocal shout becomes FM lead hook
pit breakdown becomes stage-wave combat section
industrial bass becomes controlled chip sub pressure
political lyric becomes stage title or boss concept
```

---

## Avoid

Avoid these mistakes:

```text
real band names in prompts
direct imitation
glossy modern metalcore
fantasy dragon metal
clean heroic guitar tone
festival dubstep drops
nu-metal parody
comedy-first presentation
```
