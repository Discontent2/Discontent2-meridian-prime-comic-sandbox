## External Influence Handling

This roadmap was developed from a deep-dive discussion of beloved rainlit ambient / dreampunk electronic track functions, especially the way certain landmark works build a believable emotional city through patience, haze, original synthesis, and strong sense of place.

Reference function only. Do not copy surface identifiers.


## Core Lesson

The important lesson is not simply vaporwave aesthetic.

The important lesson is:

```text
make a place feel emotionally real
```

Wet Signal District should translate that into:

```text
rain haze
slow pads
recessed pulse
emotional absence
cyclical restoration
original synth instrumentation
strong sense of place
```

## Production Rules

Use:

```text

no samples

distant muffled pulse
```

