## Watch-Specific Review

Use for KFR-COUPON-004.

- [ ] Watch is on the left wrist.
- [ ] Watch reads compact at 5-inch scale.
- [ ] Watch does not read as a wrist warmer.
- [ ] Black fingerless glove remains clearly visible.
- [ ] Velocisapien Route-Dust Olive Teal scaled skin remains visible where expected.
- [ ] No human skin reference appears.
- [ ] Watch does not block wrist swivel or hand pose.
- [ ] Watch corners, buttons, and strap are not fragile.
- [ ] No real-world branding or generated UI text is present.

---

## Pass Criteria

A coupon batch passes when:

- required joints assemble without cracking
- movement feels durable enough for careful adult prototype handling
- tail installs securely and supports balance
- left wrist watch is compact and safe to print
- garment / harness / pocket shapes do not block movement
- accessory grip holds at least one route tool
- paint / primer does not destroy fit
- no child-safe or safety-certified claims are implied
