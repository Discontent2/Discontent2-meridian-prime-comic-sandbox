## Known High-Risk Areas

| Risk ID | Area | Risk | Mitigation | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| KFR-RISK-001 | Tail | Tail too heavy / pulls figure backward. | Adjust tail angle, widen stance, use tail as third contact point. | Open |
| KFR-RISK-002 | Tail socket | Keyed socket cracks. | Increase wall thickness, generous fillets, coupon test. | Open |
| KFR-RISK-003 | Watch | Watch too large. | Use compact scale, KFR-COUPON-004, compare to glove silhouette. | Open |
| KFR-RISK-004 | Watch / glove | Watch reads like wrist warmer. | Keep hard case, screen, side buttons, strap distinct; preserve fingerless glove. | Open |
| KFR-RISK-005 | Skin | Human skin contamination. | Route-Dust Olive Teal scaled skin only. Reject human-skin references. | Open |
| KFR-RISK-006 | Jacket | Jacket blocks shoulder movement. | Trim bulk, test shoulder / jacket / harness coupon. | Open |
| KFR-RISK-007 | Pants | Cargo pockets block hip movement. | Flatten / shift pocket relief, test hip coupon. | Open |
| KFR-RISK-008 | Hands | Accessory grip fails. | Test route probe / flag / optional handheld reader handle coupon. | Open |
| KFR-RISK-009 | Quills / claws | Fragile sharp points. | Shorten, thicken, blunt, test support removal. | Open |
| KFR-RISK-010 | Paint | Paint rub destroys joint fit. | Coupon test raw / primer / paint / clear stages. | Open |
