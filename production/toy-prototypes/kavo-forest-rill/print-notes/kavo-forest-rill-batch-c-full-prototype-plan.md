---

## Purpose

This file plans Batch C: the first full Kavo "Forest" Rill prototype print after Batch A joint / grip testing and Batch B partial body assembly testing.

Batch C should produce a complete unpainted prototype suitable for raw assembly, final pre-paint review, paint-rub testing, accessory fit confirmation, and shelf-read evaluation.

---

## Batch C Rule

Do not print Batch C until Batch B has produced a **Go** or **Conditional Go** decision.

Batch C should not be used to discover basic joint tolerances, hand grips, or whether the figure can stand. Those belong to Batch A and Batch B. Batch C is for validating the revised full prototype as a complete object.

---

## Batch C Objectives

Batch C should answer:

1. Does the full figure assemble without force or cracking?
2. Does the complete figure stand in neutral and relaxed route-readiness poses?
3. Does the tail support balance, attitude, and shelf presence?
4. Do selected joints retain fit after full assembly?
5. Do hands hold all final accessories?
6. Does the figure read as Kavo at 6 feet, 3 feet, and 1 foot?
7. Are all canon markers visible?
8. Are paint zones accessible and logical?
9. Are any parts too sharp, brittle, thin, or fragile?
10. Is the prototype ready for primer and paint testing?

- elbow / knee joint experiment

## Full Assembly Test Order

Test assembly in this order:

1. Legs to pelvis.
2. Tail to pelvis.
3. Torso to pelvis.
4. Arms to torso.
5. Hands to wrists or fixed-hand arms.
6. Head to neck.
7. Accessories in hands.
8. Neutral standing pose.
9. Relaxed route-readiness pose.
10. Tail-assisted stance.
11. Full accessory loadout stance.
12. Pre-paint disassembly check.

Stop if any part risks cracking. Do not brute-force a collectible goblin.

## Go / No-Go for Paint Prototype

### Go to Paint if:

- [ ] Full prototype assembles without cracking.
- [ ] Figure stands without a required display base.
- [ ] Tail supports balance and character.
- [ ] Head expression reads correctly.
- [ ] Hands hold selected accessories.
- [ ] All required canon markers are visible.
- [ ] Paint zones are accessible.
- [ ] No required part is too fragile.
- [ ] Paint-rub samples pass or masking plan exists.

### Conditional Go if:

- [ ] Minor sanding fixes fit.
- [ ] Paint masking is required for joints.
- [ ] One accessory needs small thickness adjustment.
- [ ] Tail curve needs minor change but current version stands.

### No-Go if:

- [ ] Figure cannot assemble safely.
- [ ] Figure cannot stand.
- [ ] Tail socket or pelvis fails.
- [ ] Head read is wrong.
- [ ] Hands cannot hold accessories.
- [ ] Required canon markers are missing.
- [ ] Multiple structural parts require redesign.

Decision:

- [ ] Go to paint
- [ ] Conditional Go to paint
- [ ] No-Go, revise and reprint
