## Known Issues

- This is a procedural primitive blockout, not a hand-sculpted Blender/ZBrush/Nomad production mesh.
- The model is useful for proportions, silhouette, part placement, and review workflow only.
- Joint placeholders are visible, but actual toleranced joints have not been engineered.
- Garment folds, scale texture, refined head expression, boot sculpt, harness surface details, and watch UI are intentionally omitted.
- The route harness, jacket, and pants are broad masses only.
