## v002 Review Criteria

v002 can be **Approved for Detail Sculpt** only if:

- Kavo identity reads immediately from front / side / back / three-quarter views.
- The head has a convincing Forest expression direction.
- The watch is compact, left-wrist, and clearly not a wrist warmer.
- Garment layers read as cropped route jacket, under-layer, cargo pants, and harness.
- Tail balance is plausible and socket planning is clear.
- Boots look broad enough for freestanding display.
- Hands preserve four-finger anatomy.
- Boot fronts preserve four-toe / claw read.
- Shoulder, wrist, hip, and tail joint placeholders are plausible enough for coupon derivation.
- No hard guardrails are violated.

v002 should be **Approved with Required Fixes** if big forms work but a limited set of proportional or clearance edits remain.

v002 should be **Rejected / Rework Blockout** if Kavo reads human, generic raptor, monster, military, ninja, or if tail / stance / watch / anatomy are fundamentally wrong.

## Specific v002 Focus Notes

### Head

Refine the head before adding surface scales.

Target:

- sly
- calm
- precise
- socially awake
- dangerous without snarling

Avoid:

- monster roar
- blank lizard mask
- human nose / human skull read
- oversized sunglasses gag

### Watch

The watch must remain:

- left wrist
- compact
- hard device silhouette
- matte-black field gear in paint assumption
- compatible with fingerless glove cuff
- clear of wrist swivel planning

Reject if it becomes a cuff, wrist warmer, gauntlet, superhero device, or giant outdoor GPS brick.

### Tail

Tail must do three jobs:

1. silhouette
2. balance
3. emotion / attitude

v002 should clarify tail base and socket enough that KFR-COUPON-006 can be planned after approval.

### Garments

Garments should support toy read and articulation.

- jacket clears shoulder movement
- jacket hem clears hips and tail base
- pants pockets clear hip movement
- boot collar meets pants cuff cleanly
- no dangling straps or fragile cords
