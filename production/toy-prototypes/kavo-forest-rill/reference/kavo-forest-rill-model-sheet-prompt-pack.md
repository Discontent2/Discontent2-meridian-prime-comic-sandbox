## Shared Canon-Safe Core Description

Use this description in every prompt unless the individual asset prompt already includes it in full:

Kavo "Forest" Rill is a Velocisapien / Velociraptoroid Mountaineer, route guide, wilderness EMT, and GPR path-reader from the Meridian Prime universe. Kavo Rill is his real name. Forest Rill is his human / crew name. He has a mostly humanoid saurian body plan, expressive saurian face with snout and brow structure, scaled skin, four-fingered clawed hands, four-toed clawed feet, and a universal tail used for balance, attitude, combat posture, and emotion. He has a short sparse red proto-quill mohawk, black-frame shades as his primary toy head look, optional route goggles as an alternate look, subtle checker trim, light route harness, pressure-route badges, claw-wraps, and a relaxed posture that can become dangerous instantly.

He should feel like relaxed menace: precise, streetwise, impossible to rush, quiet until funny, funny until dangerous, dangerous until useful.
