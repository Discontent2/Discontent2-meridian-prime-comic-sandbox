## Purpose

This file records the final review of the painted Kavo "Forest" Rill prototype.

Use it after the raw Batch C prototype has passed into paint and the painted figure has completed final assembly. This review decides whether the prototype packet is complete, needs touch-up, requires a revised paint pass, or must return to print / sculpt revisions.

---

## Review Rule

Do not mark the prototype packet complete until this review confirms:

- painted figure still assembles safely
- figure still stands with final accessories
- paint preserves all required canon markers
- shelf-read succeeds at 6 feet, 3 feet, and 1 foot
- articulation and removable parts still function after paint
- accessories remain usable
- no unsafe sharp points or brittle hazards remain for adult display handling
- toy-only or candidate canon details are labeled properly

This is not a child-safety certification. It is an adult collectible prototype review.
