## Purpose

Use this checklist to review the first gray / neutral-material blockout submission before the artist/modeler begins detail sculpting.

The review should decide one of three outcomes:

- **Approved for Detail Sculpt**
- **Approved with Required Fixes**
- **Rejected / Rework Blockout**

This checklist is meant for screenshot and turntable review. It should catch proportion, silhouette, stance, garment, tail, watch, and joint problems before detail work starts hiding them under shiny sculpt confetti.

## Decision Standards

### Approved for Detail Sculpt

Use only if:

- silhouette works from front / side / back / three-quarter views
- Kavo identity is clear
- tail balance is plausible
- watch scale and placement are correct
- garment layers are clear
- joint placeholders are usable
- no hard guardrail is violated

### Approved with Required Fixes

Use if the blockout is mostly correct but needs limited edits before detailing, such as:

- watch needs 10 to 20 percent resizing
- pocket needs shifting for hip clearance
- tail needs slight angle change
- boot footprint needs widening
- jacket cuff needs trimming

### Rejected / Rework Blockout

Use if the blockout has foundational problems, such as:

- Kavo reads human or generic raptor
- watch is huge or wrist-warmer-like
- tail cannot support stance / identity
- hands / feet anatomy is wrong
- pose is sprinting or monster-like
- garment layers block articulation
- figure cannot plausibly stand
